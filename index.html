About Me

Hi! I’m Alina Bylkova and I’m a web developer working and living in beautiful Oslo.

Creating and developing things for the Internet is my greatest passion. I strive to build immersive and beautiful web sites through carefully crafted code and user-centric design. I’m obsessed with coding things from scratch, and enjoy bringing ideas to life in the browser. I always prefer to keep learning, continue challenging myself, find new opportunities in web development, and do things that matter.

Apart from coding, I enjoy playing board games, listening synthwave music and watching cyberpunk movies. Denis Villeneuve and Robert Anthony Rodriguez have made some of my most favorite films and their creativity is an inspiration for me. I love to travel and also enjoy skiing.
